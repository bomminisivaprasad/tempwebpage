The Game

Before The Brain Olympics, participants shall be provided with brain training workshops that shall cover all the techniques (spread over two workshops) that the participants may use for the Olympics.

Before the beginning of workshop 1, A pre-training assessment shall be conducted to assess the participants’ current learning levels, and post the workshops, post-training assessment shall also be conducted to assess if the participants’ score across different exercises improved. This improvement in the score shall be attributed to the skills acquisition happening as a result of the workshops.

Post workshops, participants shall be given training materials and a week to prepare for the final event : Vijayawada memory olympics. The olympics shall be held on August 13th, 2022 on the same techniques as taught before but at a harder skill & difficulty level. Participants’ score on the memory olympics (called Post-olympics scores) shall be compared with both post assessment and pre-assessment scores to evaluate and quantify net learning improvement outcomes.

About

What are the games?

Vijayawada Memory Olympics are memory games held for high school students in Vijaywada with the aim to challenge and broaden their mental horizons, build cognitive and neural feedback skills, gamify learning and exercise their brain muscles to expand on their abilities to recall concepts and connect concepts together.



Why are you having them?

Help students acquire skills such as brain training, focus, retention of concepts and concept linking.Help students be exposed to the concept of gamifying learning and expanding their ways of thinking and engaging with the content by increasing their schemas concerning the subject matter and linking different concepts together organically.These skills would be especially helpful with subjects such as History and Geography/Biology.



Where are they being held?

The games are being held in the city of Vijayawada, in the state of Andhra Pradesh, in India.
